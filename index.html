My name is Augusto Sales de Queiroz, and I am a computer engineering student at Universidade Federal de Pernambuco (UFPE). That much is easy to know. By that's probably not everything you're looking for. If you're here (and you're not), there is probably one single question in your mind: Why should I care? Well the answer to that is somewhat complicated, but perhaps one (if not some) of the things I tell you next might grab your attention.

Whilst studying computer engineering, I've already found two areas of interest for development: Game Design and Artificial intelligence. On the former, I've started bettering myself in 2015, when, I worked with a team to bring the game Pontes to life during Kolks Game Jam. The latter has been a more dificult climb, but I am currently on the 4th week of Stanford's Machine Learning Course, on Coursera, and before that is finished, will be taking the Computational Neuroscience course, and hopefully many more to come.
